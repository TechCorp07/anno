Proctoring Requirements

⚠️ 1. Camera Monitoring (REQUIRED)

Your camera must remain on throughout the entire exam. The system will:

  • Capture periodic snapshots (every 2-5 minutes)
  • Continuously monitor that your camera stays enabled
  • Alert and log if camera is turned off during exam
  • Store images securely for 30 days, then auto-delete

⚠️ WARNING: Turning off your camera during the exam will result in disqualification!

2. Screen Capture

Periodic screenshots of your screen will be captured to ensure test integrity. Screenshots are taken at the same intervals as camera snapshots.

3. Browser Restrictions

During the test:

  • Fullscreen mode is required
  • Tab switching is not allowed (max 3 warnings)
  • Right-click and keyboard shortcuts are disabled
  • Copy/paste is disabled
  • Developer tools are blocked

4. IP Address Tracking

Your IP address will be logged to detect multiple device usage.

5. Plagiarism Detection

Your answers will be compared with other test attempts to detect similarities (≥70% threshold).

Important Notes

  • Camera MUST stay on - Do not disable or cover your camera
  • Use a quiet, well-lit environment
  • Ensure your face is clearly visible to the camera
  • Do not leave the test page once started
  • Violations may result in immediate test disqualification
  • All proctoring data is automatically deleted after 30 days

⚠️ Exam Disqualification Conditions

  • Turning off camera during the exam
  • Covering the camera or blocking the view
  • Excessive tab switching (more than 3 warnings)
  • Leaving fullscreen mode repeatedly
  • Using external resources or assistance
